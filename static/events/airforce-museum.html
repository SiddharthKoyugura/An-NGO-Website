AIRFORCE MUSEUM

November 2, 2016 / By trpws2012

Aim - To make children aware of the people and institute of the country that protects us from aerial invasions and national troubles.

Air Force Museum, the only one of its kind in India, houses a rich collection of memorabilia of Indian Military Aviation and displays the history of Indian Air Force. The children got to know about the brave aircraft pilots and historical aircrafts that were used in wars and learn that sacrifices for their motherland is one of the biggest sacrifices ever committed by anyone. The love of thy country is the main feeling and emotion that the children took home with them.

Many of them jumped with excitement on the way back, exclaiming how they themselves now want to be a pilot in future and fly those magnificient beasts into the sky. The hope on their faces seemed genuine.

Country first, my colleague next and myself last was the lesson the children learnt that day. Patriotism is not trolling people but to stand in the border and live for the country.
